I'm Matthew Gatland and I used to make tiny games all the time. But then something went wrong.


I grew tired, or lazy, or caught up in everyday life. My work slowed down and stopped. It's now been 14 months since I released a game.


You can help! I don't need your money. I just need some pressure. I need a deadline, and the deadline has to matter so I can't ignore it.


So here's the deal: from now on, every month, I will release a game. But if I don't, I'll pay each of my anti-patrons 5 New Zealand dollars.


If I miss the deadline, I have to pay up.


Hopefully that will be enough pressure to force me back into action. If it's not, you'll find out in your monthy payment email!


To avoid some logistical and legal issues, payments will be in the form of digital Steam gift cards. When you sign up I'll add you as a friend on Steam. We have to be Steam friends for a few days before Steam will let me send you a gift card.
