I'm Matthew Gatland and I used to make tiny games all the time. But then something went wrong.


I got tired – or lazy – or caught up in the other parts of life. I don't know what happened. All I know is this: for the first time in my adult life, I've gone 12 months without releasing a game.


You can help! I don't need your money. I just need some pressure. I need a reason to get the job done, so I can't keep putting it off.


So here's the deal: from now on, every month, I will release a game. But if I don't, I'll pay each of my anti-patrons 5 New Zealand dollars.


If I miss the deadline, I have to pay up.


Hopefully that's enough pressure to force me back into action. If it's not, you'll find out in your monthy payment email!
